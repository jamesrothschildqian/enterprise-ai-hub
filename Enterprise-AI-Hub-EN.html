Universal Pain Points 01 & 02

Inefficient Paperwork · Inaccurate Demand Forecasting

Pain Point 01
📄 Massive Repetitive Paperwork Drives Up Labor Costs
Manual data entry is slow and error-prone; labor costs erode margins.
AI Solution
Deploy OCR + large document-parsing LLMs + RPA Digital Workers (AI Agents) to auto-identify invoices, contracts, customs declarations, expense reports, HR files, and engineering drawings. Perform structured entry, logic verification, and archiving. Generative AI drafts standard contracts, official letters, and reports in bulk.
Cross-industry Case · Yonyou BIP
Expense audit cycle: 5 working days → 8 hours. Document entry error rate: 12% → 0.8%. Mid-sized enterprises save the equivalent of 3–5 finance staff annually. AI contract parsing handles 10,000+ contracts/month, cutting manual workload by 70%.
Pain Point 02
📦 Experience-Based Forecasting Leads to Inventory Imbalance
Overstock, stockouts, misallocated inventory, and heavy capital tie-up.
AI Solution
Deploy time-series ML models (TFT/LSTM) + multi-source data fusion models integrating historical orders, industry sentiment, seasonal factors, competitor data, macro indicators, and upstream/downstream supply chain data. Dynamically forecast weekly/monthly demand and auto-generate safety stock thresholds and replenishment plans.
Cross-industry Case · AI Demand Forecasting SaaS
Adopted by Yiwu commodity traders, home appliance manufacturers, and building material procurement teams. Inventory turnover days: 42 → 19 days. Slow-moving inventory impairment losses down 45%.
05 / 19
Universal Pain Points 03 & 04

Fragmented Customer Service · High-Cost Manual Risk Control

Pain Point 03
🤖 Scattered Inquiries — No 7×24 Coverage
Overstaffed basic customer service teams; high-intent leads lost to slow follow-up.
AI Solution
Deploy multimodal LLM-powered intelligent customer service Agents to handle all-channel FAQs, auto-classify leads, initiate outbound follow-up calls, and transfer high-intent customers to human agents. Generative AI auto-generates customized business letters and property consultation scripts.
Case · Taji Futern Global AI Sales Agent
Serving foreign trade, manufacturing equipment, real estate investment, and financial services. Lead screening efficiency up 6×. Email open rate: 6% → 15%. Business inquiries tripled.
Pain Point 04
🛡 Manual Risk Control — High Fraud & Bad Debt Exposure
Compliance gaps go undetected; post-incident accountability is costly.
AI Solution
Implement risk control models combining graph neural networks, rule engines, and federated learning. Real-time monitoring of transactions, contract performance, supply chain payments, and abnormal customer qualifications. Pre-risk rating → in-process anomaly alerts → post-event analysis, all compliant with industry regulations.
Case · Group-level Integrated AI Risk Control Platform
Shared across trade, real estate, factoring, and manufacturing subsidiaries. A/R overdue early-warning accuracy: 89%. Bad debt ratio down 32% year-on-year.

Universal Pain Point 05

Severe Data Silos — Management Lacks Real-Time Decision Support

Pain Point 05
📊 Data Silos Make Reporting Time-Consuming

AI Solution

Spanning manufacturing, trade, and real estate segments. Monthly operational reports: 7 manual working days → 1 hour for full AI analysis. Data analyst headcount reduced by 50%.

Blind Overseas Client Development
Manually screening vast customs and social media data is extremely inefficient; generic outreach emails yield low open rates.

Language Barriers & Time Zone Delays Hurt Customer Response
High risk of order loss due to delayed complaint handling.
